# Harness Engineering Audit: OpenClaw v2026.3.31

**Date**: 2026-03 (golden reference)
**Profile**: monorepo | **Stage**: mature
**Ecosystem**: TypeScript/Node.js + Swift + Kotlin (multi-platform)
**Language**: English (en)

## Overall Grade: B (78.0/100)

## Executive Summary

OpenClaw v2026.3.31 represents the strongest harness state in the time series (+13.4 points from beta5 baseline). The monorepo has grown to 94 packages with robust cross-package boundary enforcement, full observability stack (tslog + OTel), CODEOWNERS, and documented rollback procedures. Mechanical constraints hit 85.7% with dependency-direction enforcement via custom boundary scripts and convention checks. Observability achieves a perfect 100% across all 5 items. The primary drag is entropy management (50%) — no tech-debt register, no formal AI-slop detection — and long-running task support (50%) — no durable execution or handoff templates. The formatter enforcement gap (2.3 FAIL: `pnpm check` does not run `oxfmt --check` despite the CI step name suggesting it does) is the most actionable quick fix.
