## Overall Grade: B (72.3/100)

## Executive Summary

OpenClaw v2026.1.30 shows significant growth from the beta5 baseline (+7.7 points). The monorepo expanded from 2 to 33 packages with a full extension architecture. Key improvements include enhanced E2E testing infrastructure (`vitest.e2e.config.ts`, Docker test scripts), OpenTelemetry integration via extensions, pre-commit hooks (`.pre-commit-config.yaml`), and secret detection in CI. The `AGENTS.md` (~178 lines) remains well under the monorepo threshold (300 lines for 33 packages). Persistent weaknesses: no `CODEOWNERS`, no `llms.txt`, coverage thresholds not CI-gated, and entropy management remains basic.
