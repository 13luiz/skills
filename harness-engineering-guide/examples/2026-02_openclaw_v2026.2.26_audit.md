## Anti-Patterns Identified

- **AGENTS.md exceeds threshold**: ~257 lines mixing repo map, architecture, testing policy, release/security, platform notes, and collaboration rules. Exceeds monorepo PASS threshold (~200) — in PARTIAL range (200-350), not yet at encyclopedia level under dynamic thresholds.
- **Broken reference**: `.agents/skills/PR_WORKFLOW.md` referenced but not present in tree — erodes "single source of truth."
- **Coverage drift**: Thresholds configured in `vitest.config.ts` but never enforced in CI.
- **Dead code CI disabled**: `deadcode` job in `ci.yml` is `if: false` (report-only lane parked).
