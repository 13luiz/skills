## Overall Grade: B (72.8/100)

## Executive Summary

OpenClaw v2026.2.26 shows steady improvement from Jan (+0.5 points), with the most notable advances in boundary enforcement and documentation. Custom boundary scripts (`scripts/check-channel-agnostic-boundaries.mjs`) represent the first dependency-direction enforcement (Dim 2.5 improves to PARTIAL). Security testing matured significantly with adversarial test cases (prompt injection, exec injection). Documentation hygiene improved with `pnpm check:docs`, markdownlint, and `.github/workflows/stale.yml`. The `AGENTS.md` (~256 lines) remains under the 300-line monorepo cap. Key gaps persist: no `llms.txt`, coverage not CI-gated, `CODEOWNERS` still absent, and entropy management remains the weakest dimension.
